A Voice Acting & Voice-Over Course

2 Days · Group

About This Program

Your Voice
Is The Instrument.

Voice Lab is a 2-day intensive that treats the human voice as the most dynamic and expressive instrument in existence — and teaches you how to use it with intention. Whether you want to do voice-over work, commercial recording, or simply become a more compelling communicator, this course builds the technical foundation.

We cover the full emotional spectrum of voice acting through live script analysis and character delivery, paired with the technical side: microphone technique, proximity effect, self-recording setups, and navigating a studio environment as a vocal talent.

You leave with a short voice-over demo recorded in a professional studio — your first portfolio piece.

Curriculum

What's Covered

01
Script Analysis

How to break down a script for intent, subtext, tone, and audience — before you ever step in front of a microphone.

02
Vocal Delivery & Range

Exercises to unlock your emotional range — warmth, authority, playfulness, urgency — and apply them with control.

03
Projection & Articulation

Breathing technique, diction, and the mechanics of a powerful, clear vocal presence both in and out of the booth.

04
Microphone Technique

Understanding proximity effect, mic placement, pop filters, and how small positional adjustments change the character of your sound.

05
Self-Recording Setup

How to build a functional home recording setup for auditions, demos, and remote voice-over work on a realistic budget.

06
Studio Demo Recording

A live, in-studio recording session where you record your voice-over demo — coached in real time and produced for your portfolio.
